About us

Roman's Lab
The activity of Roman's Lab is one of the links in the production chain of geological exploration and development of hydrocarbon deposits. The company specializes in performing a full range of expert and scientific and analytical work in the field of subsoil use and the fuel and energy complex, research of oil, gas and gas condensate wells, their trial operation, laboratory studies of reservoir fluids: gas, gas condensate, oil, water, mechanical properties of soils and core.

Our services

We provide a large number of services in different areas.
Scientific and technical center
The Scientific and Technical Center Roman's Lab deals with the issues of strategic development in the field of reproduction of the mineral resource base.
Modern equipment, innovative technologies, the use of our own software products allow our highly professional specialists to carry out a full range of expert and scientific and analytical work in the field of subsoil use and the fuel and energy complex.
Field research
Many years, Roman's Lab has been performing a complex of field gas-hydrodynamic and gas-condensate studies of oil, gas and gas-condensate wells. During this time, work has been completed at more than 10 oil and gas condensate fields, dozens wells have been explored, and vast experience and capacities have been accumulated. All work is carried out independently, without the involvement of contractors. The field research department employs more than 50 people. All specialists are trained and certified for high-quality work.
Laboratory studies of formation fluids
Laboratory and Analytical Center (LAC) Roman's Lab consists of laboratories for PVT research, chromatographic, chemical-analytical and physico-chemical studies.
The Laboratory and Analytical Center performs laboratory tests:
- PVT studies of gas condensate systems;
- PVT study of deep samples of oil, water;
- Chromatographic studies;
- Determination of physical and chemical properties of surface samples of oil, gas condensate, gas.
Laboratory research of core
Roman's Lab research of core center is a high-tech analytical center (laboratory) fully equipped with modern equipment, which takes on the solution of any research tasks in the study of rocks (core), minerals and non-metallic raw materials, formation waters. The studies give an idea of ​​the reservoir properties of rocks, the physical and chemical characteristics of oil, gas, gas condensate, formation waters used for calculating hydrocarbon reserves, designing and analyzing field development systems.
